Context

Part of Enlighten Festival 2025, projected onto the facade of the National Library of Australia. The Electric Canvas produced the projection in collaboration with Beautiful and Useful Studio, whose artwork formed the visual foundation of the piece.

The Approach

The Electric Canvas's presence at Enlighten Festival covered several buildings - among them was translating 'The Guardians', Matthew Aberline's collaboration with Beautiful and Useful Studio, onto the facade of the National Library of Australia. As design lead, I first adapted the artwork for the building facade, then developed the animated sequences using ComfyUI with a custom ControlNet pipeline and AnimatedDiff, with compositing and finishing in After Effects. Getting a show out is a whole-company effort at The Electric Canvas - from the first conversation to the night it runs.

The Result

The projection ran nightly during Enlighten Festival 2025, transforming the National Library facade into an animated canvas visible across Canberra's Parliamentary Triangle.
