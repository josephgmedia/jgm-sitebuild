The Brief

A content series for RAMS Home Loans making construction finance feel less daunting captured as bite-sized educational videos fronted by Australian TV presenter Tom Williams, set inside a world built entirely from building blocks.

The Approach

Building from a title lockup and rough frames by the lead designer, I was responsible for storyboarding the series and art directed, built and animated the 3D Lego-world environment Tom inhabits through to edit, colour and final mix. The challenge was making a playful, toy-like 3D world feel credible enough to sit alongside live action green screen footage, while holding consistency across a large suite of deliverables.
