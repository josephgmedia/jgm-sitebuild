Tools

The Brief

A how-to series for Hyundai Auto Link — a connected car product that paired a physical module in the vehicle with a smartphone app, letting owners monitor vehicle performance, driving habits and service history. The series needed a friendly guide to walk viewers through the app's features, keeping a consistent tone across multiple videos while giving each its own identity. It was later extended for a Caltex fuel promotion, with the character redesigned to align with Caltex's OOH campaign.

The Approach

I designed and animated the character host, art directed the series, and composed a full underscore for each video — individually scored but consistent in tone — to keep the series feeling continuous rather than episodic. I also engineered the VO session and mixed the final audio.
