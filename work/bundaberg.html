Bundaberg Rum — The Wedding That Fans Built (Cerumony)

Role Videographer, Edit, Colour
Client Diageo Australia / Bundaberg Rum
Agency Leo Burnett (via PXP Studios)
Year 2023
Tools
Premiere Pro DaVinci Resolve
← All Work

The Brief

A crowd-sourced wedding at the Bundaberg Rum distillery, planned entirely by the brand's 400,000 social followers. Bundaberg needed the day itself captured and turned around as a film that could carry the campaign across social and earned media.

The Approach

I came on as videographer to shoot the wedding as a real event, advising on camera gear hire ahead of the day, and handled edit and colour in post. The focus was on capturing the day cleanly and with pace — enough energy to match the campaign's tone while still reading as an actual wedding rather than an ad.

The Result

The film travelled further than most branded content ever does — picked up by Channel 7 News on location, a follow-up Channel 9 interview with the couple the next day, and 167 pieces of earned media with 100% positive sentiment. The campaign drew 24.8M earned impressions, with Facebook engagement 1,444% above industry standard and Instagram 736% above. The wider work went on to win at The One Show, the Caples Awards and the ONE Asia Creative Awards in 2024.
