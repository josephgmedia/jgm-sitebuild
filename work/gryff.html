The Brief

Gryff approached me wanting a music video for his 80s synthwave single "Losing Touch" featuring Max Cruise - something cathartic and cinematic, leaning into synthwave and 80s neon visuals with the feel of a late-night drive.

The Approach

I pitched a director's treatment and we shot over two nights across Sydney - a rooftop, driving through a city tunnel in a hired Ford Cortina, and performance shots through Chinatown. I produced the shoot, shot as DOP, edited and graded.
