Music Video

Gryff — Losing Touch (ft. Max Cruise)

Role Producer, DOP, Edit, Colour
Client Gryff
Year 2024
Tools
Premiere Pro DaVinci Resolve
← All Work

The Brief

Australian artist Gryff approached me wanting a music video for his 80s synthwave single "Losing Touch" featuring Max Cruise - something cathartic and cinematic, leaning into synthwave and 80s neon visuals with the feel of a late-night drive.

The Approach

I pitched a director's treatment built around Sydney's neon-lit cityscape. We shot over two nights across the city: rooftop performance, tunnel driving sequences in a hired Ford Cortina, and street shots through Chinatown. I handled production, shot as DOP, and completed edit and grade.

The Result

The video delivered the 80s synthwave aesthetic Gryff wanted while staying grounded in Sydney's urban landscape, translating the nostalgic Australian track into a cohesive visual narrative.
