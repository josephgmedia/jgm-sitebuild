The Brief

A collaboration piece between cosmetic dentist Dr Jack and Porsche, featuring an Iron Man-style den scene between Dr Jack and his virtual assistant - holographic displays and 3D interactivity.

The Approach

I designed and animated the UI, motion tracked the plate and replaced a placeholder desk with a 3D tech heavy table, animated 3D elements corresponding to character input - then composited and graded the shot to final.
