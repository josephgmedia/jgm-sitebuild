I don't compartmentalise well — which means I bring music thinking to motion problems, illustration thinking to code problems, and parenting thinking to client communication.

I'm a Senior Motion Designer at The Electric Canvas, a Sydney-based architectural projection company. Before that: Tonic Films, OMD, Saatchi & Saatchi — press packages, advertising, large-scale media conferences, cinema formats, OOH.

Outside of that I'm a freelance creative, self-published children's book author and illustrator, a recording musician, and a dad of two boys. I started opening Photoshop before I knew what I was doing. That instinct hasn't gone away.
