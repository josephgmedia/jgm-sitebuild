A hunger to learn everything and refusing to stop at one changes the way I see the world - music informs how I approach motion, and parenting informs how I approach clients.

I'm currently a Senior Motion Designer at The Electric Canvas, with a decade of experience across Saatchi & Saatchi, OMD and various production companies - working in large-scale architectural projection, advertising, media, broadcast, social, live event production, cinema formats and OOH.

Outside of that I'm a freelance creative, a self-published children's book author and illustrator, a recording musician, and a dad of two boys. I started opening Photoshop before I knew what I was doing. That instinct hasn't gone away.
