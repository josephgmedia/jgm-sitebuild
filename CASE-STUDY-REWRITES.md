## 2. NLA Enlighten

### The Approach (REVISED - Team-focused but shows your lead role)
As design lead, I worked with The Electric Canvas team to translate Beautiful and Useful Studio's artwork into a large-scale projection. Using AI animation workflows (ComfyUI and AnimatedDiff), I directed the transformation of static illustrations into seamless looping sequences, composited in After Effects. TEC's projection team handled the technical mapping and on-site installation.

### The Result (NEW - ADD THIS)
The projection ran nightly during Enlighten Festival 2025, transforming the National Library facade into an animated canvas visible across Canberra's Parliamentary Triangle.
